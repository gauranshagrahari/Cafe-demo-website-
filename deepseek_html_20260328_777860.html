Our Story

Brew & Co. began as a passion for community and craft coffee. Tucked in the heart of the city, we serve ethically sourced beans, homemade pastries, and a warm space where every detail is made for connection.

Chef Elena Rossi

Former patissier from Milan, Elena brings authentic Italian love to our bakery and brunch menu. Her signature tiramisu and sourdough have become local legends.
